My Role

UX researcher and designer

Background

Many people experience FOMO (fear of missing out) when they find out that their favorite artists played live shows near them and they didn't know about it. This project set out to interview the people who often go to live shows, how they find these shows, and how a service could improve upon their current workflow.

Goal

Design an app/service that could help music lovers everywhere learn about upcoming shows in their area, the venues that the show will take place in, as well as any other relevant information such as venue amenities or restrictions (COVID-related or otherwise).

Research Objectives

  1. Understand how music lovers currently keep up with live events that are happening near them and what their pain points are with their current process.
  2. Find out what users want to know about the live music events happening around them and the venues they are hosted on.

End Result

An app with simple onboarding that links with the user's music app of choice and displays upcoming shows in their area. On the event page, they can see information about buying tickets as well as details about the venue, such as: coat check availability, parking space, COVID requirements, among other things.
