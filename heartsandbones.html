Hearts & Bones Website Redesign

Website redesign proposal for Hearts & Bones dog rescue in NYC focused on revamping the aesthetics and adding functionality.

My Role

UX researcher and designer

Background

Hearts & Bones Rescue provides a great service to its community but their website leaves a lot to be desired.

Goal

Design an app/service that could help music lovers everywhere learn about upcoming shows in their area, the venues that the show will take place in, as well as any other relevant information such as venue amenities or restrictions (COVID-related or otherwise).

Research Objectives

  1. Understand the needs of people who are looking to adopt dogs and how the website can help them achieve that goal.
  2. Why or why not they have chosen to adopt online
  3. Why and how are they choosing to adopt from their chosen rescue
  4. What questions did users have during the adoption process
  5. In an ideal world, what would the adoption process look like?

End Result

An app with simple onboarding that links with the user's music app of choice and displays upcoming shows in their area. On the event page, they can see information about buying tickets as well as details about the venue, such as: coat check availability, parking space, COVID requirements, among other things.
